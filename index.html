Questions

Frequently Asked

We offer free rescheduling or cancellation if there's severe weather. Light rain? We provide free ponchos and many riders love the trails in misty conditions! If you choose to return early due to rain, we'll prorate your rental.

Every rental includes a puncture repair kit and pump. If you can't fix it, call our support line — we'll dispatch assistance or direct you to the nearest station for a bike swap at no charge.

Walk-ins are welcome, but we strongly recommend booking ahead especially on weekends and holidays. Our premium road bikes and e-bikes often sell out 2-3 days in advance.

Riders must be 18+ to rent independently. Teens 16-17 can rent with a parent/guardian present. We have kids' bikes suitable for ages 4+ which must be rented by an adult.

Yes! We offer free multi-station returns. Just let us know at pickup if you plan to return elsewhere so we can coordinate our fleet.

Basic liability is included. Full coverage insurance (damage, theft, medical) is available as an add-on starting at $3/day depending on bike type. We highly recommend it for premium bikes.
